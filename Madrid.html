Madrid Travel Guide

Local Attractions

  • Prado Museum: one of the world's premier art museums, featuring works by Goya, Velázquez, and El Greco.
  • Retiro Park: a beautiful green space with gardens, a lake, and plenty of walking paths.
  • Plaza Mayor: a historic square with cafes, shops, and street performers.
  • Reina Sofia Museum: a modern art museum featuring works by Picasso, Dalí, and Miró.
  • Debod Temple: an ancient Egyptian temple located in the heart of the city.
