Nice Travel Guide

Nice, the capital of the French Riviera, is a popular tourist destination known for its stunning beaches, historic architecture, and vibrant cultural scene.

Local Attractions

  • Chagall Museum: A museum dedicated to the works of Marc Chagall, featuring his paintings, stained glass windows, and mosaics.
  • Promenade des Anglais: A scenic pedestrian walkway along the Baie des Anges, offering stunning views of the Mediterranean Sea.
  • Cours Saleya Market: A bustling marketplace selling fresh produce, flowers, and souvenirs.
  • Nice Cathedral: A beautiful Baroque church built in the 17th century, featuring intricate stone carvings and stunning stained glass windows.
  • Castle Hill: A historic hill offering breathtaking views of the city and the sea, with a beautiful park and a 16th-century castle.
