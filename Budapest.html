Budapest Travel Guide

Introduction to Budapest

Budapest, the capital city of Hungary, is a must-visit destination for any traveler. With its rich history, stunning architecture, and vibrant cultural scene, Budapest has something to offer for everyone.

Local Attractions

  • Buda Castle: A historic castle complex that offers stunning views of the city
  • Széchenyi Thermal Bath: One of the largest and most popular thermal baths in Europe
  • St. Stephen's Basilica: A beautiful basilica that offers panoramic views of the city from its dome
  • Chain Bridge: A iconic bridge that connects the two parts of the city
  • Andrassy Avenue: A UNESCO World Heritage Site that features stunning architecture and beautiful parks
