Rome Travel Guide

Local Attractions:

  • Colosseum: This ancient amphitheater is one of Rome's most iconic landmarks and a must-visit for anyone interested in history.
  • Pantheon: This ancient temple is a masterpiece of Roman architecture and features an impressive dome.
  • Trevi Fountain: This beautiful baroque fountain is one of the city's most famous landmarks and is said to grant wishes to those who throw a coin into the water.
  • Vatican City: The center of the Catholic Church, Vatican City is home to numerous iconic landmarks, including St. Peter's Basilica and the Sistine Chapel.
  • Roman Forum: This ancient ruin is a treasure trove of history and features numerous ancient temples, basilicas, and markets.
