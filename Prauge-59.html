Prague Travel Guide

Introduction to Prague

Prague, the capital city of the Czech Republic, is a beautiful and historic city that offers a unique blend of medieval architecture, cultural attractions, and vibrant nightlife.

Local Attractions

  • Charles Bridge: A historic bridge lined with statues and offering stunning views of the Vltava River.
  • Old Town Square: A vibrant public square surrounded by historic buildings, including the famous Astronomical Clock.
  • Prague Castle: A large castle complex that houses several museums, gardens, and churches, including St. Vitus Cathedral.
  • Petrin Tower: A steel tower that offers panoramic views of the city.
  • Wenceslas Square: A large public square that is home to several important buildings, including the National Museum and the statue of Saint Wenceslas.
