Rome Travel Page

Local Attractions:

Colosseum

The largest amphitheater in the Roman Empire, built in the 1st century AD.

Pantheon

A magnificently preserved ancient temple, built in 126 AD.

Trevi Fountain

A beautiful baroque fountain, built in 1762, where you can make a wish by throwing a coin into the water.

Vatican City

An independent city-state within Rome, home to the Pope and the center of the Catholic Church.

Roman Forum

A historic site with ancient ruins, including temples, basilicas, and markets.
